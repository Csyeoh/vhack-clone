About

Let Us Start Hacking!

Varsity Hackathon

, is the annual flagship event of the USM Computer Science Society, currently held for the third consecutive year. It's a significant student-led hackathon in Malaysia, attracting participants from both local and international universities. The event focuses on fostering innovation, collaboration, and creativity among university students, providing a platform for them to explore cutting-edge technologies and transform their ideas into projects.

Building upon the success of V HACK 2022 and 2023, this year's edition aims to enhance the experience for participants by collaborating with the Securities Commission’s FIKRA ACE-EXCEL. The event will showcase informative workshops, experienced speakers, dedicated mentors, and a prize pool up to RM37,500.

Similar to the previous year, the preliminary rounds will be held online , culminating in the grand finals at Penang , where the top teams will compete in person . The goal is to continue raising the bar for international student-led hackathons in Malaysia.

V HACK 2024 Timeline

Lost when exploring?

See the timeline below!

1. Analyse Case Studies

Review the case studies based on the challenges aligned with SDGs and analyse the problem.

2. Attend Workshops

Join a series of insightful workshops covering topics such as ideation, business thinking, introduction to Capital Market and Fintech Industry, designing, prototyping as well as pitching.

3. Formulate a Solution & Develop Front-End

Ideate and formulate new and innovative solutions to address the issues presented in the case studies and implement the front-end for your solution to be submitted during preliminary round.

4. Preliminary Submission Requirement

Submit your GitHub link for source code and presentation video for the preliminary round submission.

5. Develop the Back-End & Refine Your Solution

Top 15 teams with the best solutions will be shortlisted and are tasked to develop the back-end of your solution and to refine your overall solution to be submitted once again.

6. Pitch Your Solution

Top 15 teams will pitch your solution live physically with the opportunity to be noticed by potential investors, collaborators or employers.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is V HACK 2024?

Varsity Hackathon (V HACK) 2024 is a student-led international hackathon for local and international undergraduates to compete by formulating the best solutions to solve SDGs challenges.

When will the registration be opened for V HACK 2024?

From 27 February 2024, 3.00PM until 17 March 2024, 11.59PM (GMT+8).

What is the timeline for V HACK 2024?

1. The Preliminary Round starts once participants are successfully registered, which is on 27th of February until 24th of March. 2. However, do note that registration will be closed on 17th March 2024, if you register late, you will only have a week to develop your solution. Hence, it is best to register as early as you can so you can start your project earlier. 3. The Top 15 teams will then be announced on the 2nd of April to qualify for the next stage, Grand Final of V HACK 2024. 4. In the Grand Final of V HACK 2024, the Top 15 teams will continue to develop their solutions and submit their project on the 19th of April. 5. Then, the Top 15 will attend the one-day Grand Final of V HACK 2024 that will be held physically on the 20th of April for pitching.

Is V HACK 2024 free of charge?

Yes, it is completely free!
